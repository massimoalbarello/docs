## Digital Self

A Digital Self is the most accurate digital representation of a user.

Imagine being able to express who you are, what you like and what you are looking for online. The Digital Self empowers you to do so, at your own terms. Fabric is creating a personalized Internet where companies sign your own privacy policy and you decide who can access what. The Digital Self makes this entremely simple for users so that users can enjoy hyperpersonalized experiences in just a few clicks.
